Frequently Asked Questions

How can solar energy benefit my business?

Solar energy can significantly reduce your business’s energy costs while lowering your carbon footprint. At Solatec, we provide high-quality solar panels and comprehensive solutions that help businesses transition to renewable energy, ensuring long-term savings and a positive environmental impact.

What does Solatec’s end-to-end solar solution include?

Our end-to-end solar solutions cover everything from design and manufacturing of solar panels to installation and ongoing maintenance. We handle every step to ensure a seamless transition to solar energy, tailored to your business’s unique needs.

How long does the solar installation process take?

The installation timeline depends on the size and complexity of your project. Typically, for most businesses, the process takes 2-4 weeks from design approval to completion. Our team ensures minimal disruption to your operations during installation.

How much can I save by switching to solar energy?

Savings vary based on your energy usage and system size, but many businesses see a 50-70% reduction in energy costs. During your free consultation, we’ll provide a detailed savings estimate tailored to your business.

What happens if my solar system needs repairs?

Solatec provides dedicated customer support for all our solar systems. If repairs are needed, our team will respond promptly to diagnose and fix the issue, ensuring minimal downtime for your business.

Can Solatec help with solar panel recycling?

Yes, as part of our commitment to sustainability, we offer guidance on solar panel recycling at the end of their lifecycle. We partner with certified recycling programs to ensure environmentally responsible disposal.
